What are AI Image Tools?

AI image tools use artificial intelligence to generate, edit, upscale, and manipulate images. Text-to-image generators like Midjourney and DALL-E create images from text descriptions. AI editors can remove backgrounds, objects, or enhance photos automatically. Design platforms like Canva integrate AI throughout the creative workflow. ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI image tools?
AI image tools use artificial intelligence to generate, edit, upscale, and manipulate images. They include text-to-image generators like Midjourney and DALL-E, editing tools that can remove backgrounds or objects, and design platforms with AI features like Canva.
Which AI image generator is best?
Midjourney leads in artistic quality and aesthetics. DALL-E 3 excels at prompt understanding and text rendering. Stable Diffusion offers the most flexibility as open source. Ideogram is best for typography and logos. Flux is the new contender with Midjourney-level quality.
Can I use AI-generated images commercially?
It depends on the tool. Midjourney, DALL-E, and Adobe Firefly allow commercial use with paid plans. Stable Diffusion depends on the specific model license. Always check the terms of service. Adobe Firefly is specifically trained on licensed content for commercial safety.
