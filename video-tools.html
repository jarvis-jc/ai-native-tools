What are AI Video Tools?

AI video tools use artificial intelligence to generate, edit, and enhance video content. Text-to-video generators like Runway and Sora create video clips from text descriptions. Image-to-video tools animate still images. AI avatar platforms like HeyGen and Synthesia create talking-head videos with digital presenters. AI editing tools automate cuts, effects, subtitles, and enhancements. These tools are revolutionizing content creation, marketing, and training video production.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI video tools?
AI video tools use artificial intelligence to generate, edit, and enhance video content. They include text-to-video generators like Runway and Sora, AI avatar platforms like HeyGen, and video editing tools with AI features for automatic cuts, effects, and enhancements.
Which AI video generator is best?
Runway Gen-3 Alpha leads in quality and control. Sora by OpenAI shows impressive realism but has limited access. Pika is fastest and most accessible. Kling from China rivals top models. For AI avatars, HeyGen and Synthesia are industry leaders.
Can I use AI-generated videos commercially?
Yes, most paid tools (Runway, HeyGen, Synthesia) allow commercial use. Check each platform's terms. Be cautious about generating content with real people's likenesses without permission, as this can have legal implications.
